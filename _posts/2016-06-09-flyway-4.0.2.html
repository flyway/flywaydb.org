--- layout: blog subtitle: "Flyway 4.0.2 Released" permalink: /blog/flyway-4.0.2 ---

After having launched Flyway 4.0 and Flyway 4.0.1, today we are proud to announce the release of Flyway 4.0.2, which comes with a number of fixes and updates including a newer MariaDB JDBC driver for the command-line tool that resolves a number of MySQL connectivity issues.

You can find all the details in the release notes.

Enjoy Flyway 4.0.2 and grab it while it's hot!

All users are encouraged to upgrade.

Flyway is brought to you with by Axel Fontaine, Boxfuse and the many contributors.
